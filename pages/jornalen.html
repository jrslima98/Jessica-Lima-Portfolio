Newspaper

613/5000 > Making an illustration for a newspaper requires some care due to the low weight of the paper as well as the use of black / white printing.
With attention to these facts, the illustration would be developed in only one color and with few details.
The news chosen was about the pollution of two Portuguese thermoelectric plants.
I wanted something that would impact and help change habits. After all, the plants exist because there are people who consume energy. In other words, the responsibility belongs to all of us.
The three illustrations were developed. All based on the same concept.
